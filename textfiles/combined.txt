
SRM Institute of Science and Technology is a top-ranked private university in India, accredited with an A++ Grade by NAAC (valid from June 16, 2024, to June 15, 2031). It holds UGC Category I University status with 12B recognition and has received accreditations from esteemed organizations such as the Institution of Engineering and Technology (IET), ABET for Engineering & Computing programs, the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) for multiple programs, and the Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR). In terms of global rankings, SRMIST is placed between 1001-1200 in QS World University Rankings and 301-350 in QS Asia Rankings. It holds 17th place in QS India Sustainability Rankings, 162nd in Asia, and 629th globally. The Times Higher Education (THE) World Rankings position it between 1201-1500, while its THE Asia ranking is 401-500. Subject-wise, Engineering & Technology, Computer Science, and Clinical & Health Sciences are ranked between 601-800. Nationally, in the NIRF India Rankings 2024, SRMIST secured 12th place in the University category, 13th in Engineering, 7th in Dental, 11th in Architecture and Pharmacy, 18th in Medical, 21st overall, and 24th in Research. The institution has also made significant research achievements, with over 920 patents filed, 47,000+ publications, and 33,000+ Scopus-indexed papers. It has secured more than ₹250 crores in external funded projects and holds the 2nd rank among private universities in the Nature Index. 

SRMIST has received several special recognitions, including 1st place in AICTE’s Swachh Campus Rankings (2019) and Clean & Smart Campus Awards, as well as 2nd place in the ARIIA Innovation Ranking 2020. The university has multiple campuses, with its main campus in Kattankulathur, Chennai, and additional locations in Ramapuram, Vadapalani, and Tiruchirappalli. It also operates SRM University - AP, SRM University - Haryana, SRM University - Sikkim, and SRM University - Delhi-NCR. 

Overall, SRMIST is a globally competitive institution with A++ NAAC accreditation, strong NIRF & QS rankings, and extensive research and innovation output. With international recognition and ABET, NBA, IET, and ICAR accreditations, it stands as a premier university committed to excellence.

SRM Institute of Science and Technology has released the hostel details for the academic year 2025-26 for the Kattankulathur Main Campus. Students are required to adhere to the hostel rules and code of conduct, and hostel fees once paid are non-refundable. The reopening dates for hostels will be announced later. Students who have booked rooms for the academic year 2025-26 can store their belongings in the cloakroom. Any issues related to booking can be resolved at the respective hostel reception desks, and the booking process can be completed online through the student portal at https://sp.srmist.edu.in/srmiststudentportal.  

Before booking, students should check their bank account's single transaction limit, read the hostel rules and code of conduct, and ensure a stable internet connection. During booking, they should log in to the student portal using a single device, check the payment transaction log in case of issues, and select the hostel block and room type without waiting for a specific room number. After booking, students need to bring essential documents, including the hostel room allocation order, two passport-sized photos, and a filled undertaking and declaration form, which will be sent to their registered email a week before reopening.  

The hostel booking schedule is as follows: second-year boys can book on February 11, 2025, at 10:00 AM, third-year boys on February 12, 2025, at 5:00 PM, and fourth and fifth-year boys on February 17, 2025, at 5:00 PM.  

The fee structure for first-year boys varies based on accommodation type. An AC room with an attached washroom and three-sharing costs ₹1,60,000, plus ₹73,000 for the mess and an optional ₹7,500 for laundry, totaling ₹2,33,000 (excluding laundry). An AC room with a common washroom and four- or five-sharing costs ₹1,15,000, bringing the total to ₹1,88,000 (excluding laundry). A non-AC room with an attached washroom and three-sharing costs ₹80,000, making the total ₹1,53,000 (excluding laundry), while a non-AC room with a common washroom and three-sharing is ₹50,000, totaling ₹1,23,000 (excluding laundry). The lowest-cost option is a non-AC room with a common washroom and four- or five-sharing, priced at ₹45,000, making the total ₹1,18,000 (excluding laundry).  

The second-year boys' hostel fee structure is identical to that of the first-year hostel. For third-year boys, the pricing remains the same as the second-year fees except for the non-AC common washroom four- or five-sharing option, which remains at ₹1,18,000. For fourth- and fifth-year boys, the non-AC attached washroom two-sharing costs ₹62,000, plus ₹73,000 for the mess, bringing the total to ₹1,35,000 (excluding laundry). A non-AC common washroom two-sharing or single-sharing room costs ₹52,000, making the total ₹1,25,000 (excluding laundry). The most expensive option is an AC attached washroom three-sharing room, costing ₹1,45,000, with a mess fee of ₹1,25,000, bringing the total to ₹2,70,000 (excluding laundry).  

For further details regarding hostel regulations, students can refer to the hostel rules at https://webstor.srmist.edu.in/web_assets/downloads/2024/srm-hostel-rules-2025.pdf and the code of conduct at https://www.srmist.edu.in/policies/code-of-conduct-for-students/. Hostel bookings can be made at https://sp.srmist.edu.in/srmiststudentportal.  

The SRMJEE application process for B.Tech and M.Tech (Integrated) programs involves several steps. Applicants must first register on the official SRMIST Application Portal at https://applications.srmist.edu.in/btech. After registration, they need to verify their email, fill out the application form online, and upload the required documents, including 10th and 12th mark sheets and a valid ID proof. The final step is to pay the non-refundable application fee of ₹1400 per phase.
